Privacy Policy Last updated: April 6, 2026 CryingJournal ("we", "our", or "the app") is an emotional wellness journaling application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information. Data Collection & Storage CryingJournal stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers. Your journal entries, photos, audio recordings, and videos remain entirely on your iPhone or iPad. Specifically, the following data is stored only on your device: Journal entries (date, intensity, duration, reason, notes) Photos attached to entries Audio recordings (voice notes) Video recordings App preferences (name, language, theme, notification settings) Biometric Data CryingJournal offers an optional Face ID / Touch ID app lock feature. Biometric authentication is handled entirely by Apple's LocalAuthentication framework. We never access, store, or transmit your biometric data. The biometric check happens on-device through Apple's secure enclave. Subscriptions & Payments CryingJournal offers optional premium subscriptions managed through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. Subscription details: Monthly: $1.99/month (auto-renewable) Yearly: $19.99/year (auto-renewable) You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. Notifications If you enable notifications, CryingJournal sends local reminders from your device. These notifications are not sent through any external server. No notification data leaves your device. Analytics & Tracking CryingJournal does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We do not collect usage statistics, crash reports, or behavioral data. Your journaling habits are completely private. Data Export You can export your data as CSV or PDF from the app's Settings. Exported files are generated locally and shared through iOS's native share sheet. We have no access to your exported files. Children's Privacy CryingJournal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used the app, please contact us. Data Deletion Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app. Uninstalling CryingJournal permanently removes all journal entries, media, and preferences from your device. Third-Party Services CryingJournal uses only the following Apple frameworks: StoreKit (for in-app subscriptions via Apple) UserNotifications (for local reminders) LocalAuthentication (for Face ID / Touch ID) WidgetKit (for home screen widget) No third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks are integrated. Changes to This Policy We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
